Jun 2018
Industry 4.0 & Additive Manufacturing Workshop

Workshop under I4.0India@IISc on Industry 4.0 & Additive Manufacturing at SSCU Auditorium, IISc, with 100+ participants from academia, PSUs, and industry leaders.

As part of the Smart Manufacturing CEFC initiative I4.0India@IISc, IISc conducted a workshop on Industry 4.0 & Additive Manufacturing Technologies” on Saturday 30th June 2018 at SSCU Auditorium, IISc, which hosted around 100 participants from academia and various Indian manufacturing and PSU/defence sectors like TCS, Bosch, Wipro, Ashok Leyland, Faurecia, GE, BEML etc. The focus of the workshop was on recent challenges, opportunities and trends on crucial areas of Industry 4.0 and Additive Manufacturing, where leaders from industry and academia came together to discuss these and charted the future course of action.
