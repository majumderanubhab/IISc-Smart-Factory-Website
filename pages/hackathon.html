About the Event

TechSprint 4.0

The National Hackathon Series 2025-26 is a flagship initiative supported by the Ministry of Heavy Industries (MHI), Government of India and Tata Steel, and organised by Indian Institute of Science.

The series aims to identify and nurture innovative technology-based solutions that will help Indian Manufacturing Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) accelerate digital transformation and the adoption of Industry 4.0 technologies.

How is this Hackathon different?

There are no "winners". All promising teams will be selected for incubation.

Guidelines

Hackathon Rules

Everything you need to know about participation requirements and guidelines

  • All participants must be aged over 18 years at the time of registration
  • Educational Qualification: Must hold at least a bachelor's degree from a recognized university or institution
  • Open to professionals, researchers, and entrepreneurs
  • Each participant must register individually through the official portal
  • All required documents must be submitted at the time of application
  • Teams of 2-3 members will be formed during the hackathon
  • Team formation happens through interaction sessions at the event
  • Each team will initially select three problem statements from the published list
  • Organizers will evaluate and allocate one problem statement to each team

Important Note

The detailed hackathon schedule, including all stages and timings, will be shared with registered participants. Make sure to complete your registration and submit all required documents before the deadline.
